Introduction

In this task, we can choose one of the Fora Form’s chair and make an incremental design for it. In this case, I chose the very classic popcorn chair.

Design brief

Solar popcorn is an incremental upgrading of the classic popcorn chair, it adopts thin-film solar panel as the backrest and sitting cushion of the chair to provide power for charging a cellphone. Besides, Solar popcorn also contain different modular sitting cushion (the user can change it by themselves) such as heating cushion, so the user can also make use of solar energy to heat the sets in winter. Solar-Popcorn also Transfers “hanging clothes on the chair’s backrest“ this unconscious behavior to a visible design by cutting two gaps on the edge of the backrest, so the user can hang their clothes on the backrest without ruining the collar.

Detail the project

After comparing the original concepts, I chose to combine the solar popcorn and unconscious hanger. Based on that, I did some testing and research.

Thin-film Solar Panel

The problem is normal solar panel is hard and straight which is not suitable for using in the chair. After some research, I found there is a material called Thin-film Solar Panel which is a soft version of solar panel.

Leaking hole

Adding solar panel to the popcorn chair will also attach some outdoor factors on the chair, then the problem is how much degree of outdoor the chair should be, should it be a pure outdoor chair? Or should it be a chair which can be both used inside and outside?
