GuardRailer

Design brief

In this design, the cleaner hangs on the highway guardrail of “W” shape. Then the cleaner can clean the highway guardrail without people’s efforts. What’s more, the cleaner doesn’t occupy any highway track. In addition, the cleaner will adopt nozzle with high-temperature steam, which contributes to saving much water.
